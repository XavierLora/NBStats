Version 1.0

March 26, 2024

We're thrilled to announce the release of version 1.0 of our NBA Stats Website! This update marks a significant milestone in our journey to provide the most comprehensive and visually appealing UI. Below are the key features included in this release:

Home Page:

- List of Upcoming Games as well as Live Game scores and Post Game detailes

- MVP and Rookie of the Year Ladder

- Top players and Teams in the League

- Post Game Data resets every day at 11 AM EST

Teams Page:

- List of Teams, their current rank within division and W-L Ratio

- Full Team stats including Pts, Ast, and Rebs

Live Page:

- List of Upcoming Games as well as Live Game scores and Post Game detailes

- Summary Stats Pre-Game

- Full Team Stats Live and Post Game

We're committed to continuously improving our NBA Stats Website to meet the needs of our users. Your feedback is invaluable to us, so please don't hesitate to reach out to NBStats2024@gmail.com with any suggestions or concerns.
